### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment in the program is extremely dynamic and friendly. Members are always enthusiastic about helping each other overcome challenges. Although it operates mainly online, the environment remains highly cohesive and effective.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The mentors and admins closely follow the internship progress, providing meticulous guidance on technical deployments. I was particularly impressed by how they suggested approaches to problems, encouraging me to think independently rather than just giving ready-made answers.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The knowledge of networking, security, and software architecture I learned at university was directly applied to this project. Integrating these concepts with Cloud technologies made the theory much more practical and vivid.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
Through the internship, I became familiar with and proficient in using AWS services and Serverless deployment skills. Additionally, I improved my time management abilities and learned how to write professional technical documentation.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
Team spirit was clearly demonstrated as everyone discussed issues, fixed bugs, and shared new knowledge together. The work culture is very open, with no barrier between interns and mentors.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The program provides interns with AWS accounts for hands-on practice without worrying about costs. Alongside this, the high-quality internal training sessions offered immense value.

**7. What did you find most satisfying during your internship?**  
Learning a lot of new knowledge about Cloud technologies, the active and enthusiastic support from the admins throughout the program, and especially the weekly events that helped continuously build and refine my skills.
